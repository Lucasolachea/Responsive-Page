About the G6

The Motorola Moto G6 is an advanced and comprehensive Android smartphone at all points of view with some excellent features. It has a large screen of 5.7 inches with a resolution of 2160x1080 pixels. The features offered by the Motorola Moto G6 are many and innovative. Starting with the LTE 4G that allows data transfer and excellent navigation on the internet. We emphasize the internal memory of 32 GB with the possibility of expansion. The 12-megapixel camera that allows the Motorola Moto G6 to take photos with a resolution of 4163x3122 pixels and record videos in high definition (Full HD) with a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels is respectable. Very thin, 8.3mm, which makes the Motorola Moto G6 really interesting.
